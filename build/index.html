About Us

At MMF Group, we see customer acquisition as a battle fought on three fronts: precision targeting, cutting-edge conversion optimization, and endless improvement of marketing spend.

This is why we apply big data analytics to everything we do — to achieve deeper customer segmentation and profiling, to hone in on your ideal customers when their interest is at it’s peak, and to tailor communications so they speak directly to their needs.

With decades of combined experience in the financial services sector and data-driven insights at the foundation of our methodology, we can help you get to know your customers better, craft customized messages, and dramatically reduce your cost per acquisition.
